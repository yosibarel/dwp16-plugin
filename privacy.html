Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the DWP-16 Plugin Privacy Policy. As an independent developer, I prioritize your privacy and data protection. You can use my plugin and visit the website without providing personal information. However, certain services, like license activation or purchases, require the collection and processing of personal data. In such cases, your consent will be requested before processing.

1. Definitions

This Privacy Policy uses the following terms:

  • Personal Data: Any information that identifies an individual, such as a name, email, or device ID.
  • Data Subject: The individual whose personal data is being processed.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, or deletion.
  • Controller: The individual (myself, as the developer) responsible for determining how and why personal data is processed.

2. Controller Contact Information

For any questions about your data or this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Independent Developer
Email: dwp@dwp-plugin.com

3. Data Collection

Data collected may include:

  • Basic Information: Name and email address for account creation or license registration.
  • Device Data: Unique device identifiers (e.g., Device ID) for license validation.
  • Payment Information: Collected by Paddle for purchase and license management.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics for improving the plugin's performance and user experience.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Data is used for:

  • Activating and managing licenses.
  • Providing technical support and updates.
  • Improving plugin functionality and user experience.
  • Complying with legal and tax obligations.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing of data is based on:

  • Your consent.
  • The need to fulfill a purchase or license agreement.
  • Legal obligations (e.g., tax reporting).
  • Legitimate interests, such as ensuring proper functionality and security.

6. Data Sharing

I work with trusted third parties to provide essential services:

  • Paddle: Handles payment processing and license management. Their privacy policy is available here.
  • Technical Tools: Third-party tools may be used for error reporting and analytics, ensuring your data is anonymized when possible.

These third parties are required to protect your data and use it solely for the agreed purposes.

7. Data Security

I implement industry-standard measures to protect your data against unauthorized access. However, please note that no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. After this period, data is securely deleted.

9. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data.
  • Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure of data ("Right to be forgotten").
  • Objection to specific data processing.
  • Portability of your data to another service provider.
  • Withdrawal of consent for data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the email provided above.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this website, and significant changes will be communicated directly to users.
