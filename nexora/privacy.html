Welcome to the Nexora Plugin Privacy Policy. As an independent developer, I prioritize your privacy and data protection. You can use my audio effect plugin and visit the website without providing personal information. 1. Definitions

This Privacy Policy uses the following terms:

  • Personal Data: Any information that identifies an individual, such as a name, email, or device ID.
  • Data Subject: The individual whose personal data is being processed.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, or deletion.
  • Controller: The individual (myself, as the developer) responsible for determining how and why personal data is processed.

Data is used for:

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing of data is based on:

All third parties I work with are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it solely for the agreed-upon purposes. Your data will never be sold or shared for marketing purposes without your consent.

I implement industry-standard measures to protect your data against unauthorized access. However, please note that no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. After this period, data is securely deleted.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the email provided above.
