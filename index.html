About DWP-16

The DWP-16 is an advanced multi-channel sampler designed for music producers, DJs, and audio enthusiasts who want to elevate their creative abilities. With 16 independent channels, each packed with powerful features and tools, DWP-16 offers complete control over every sample, effect, and parameter. Whether you are crafting beats, creating complex soundscapes, or designing custom instruments, DWP-16 provides the flexibility and functionality you need to make your ideas a reality.

Powerful Sample Editing

Each of the 16 channels in the DWP-16 can hold a unique sample, allowing for complex and layered compositions. The advanced sample editing features include pitch shifting, reverse playback, looping, and even FM synthesis on the sample itself. These tools allow you to transform any sound into something entirely new and unexpected. You can manipulate audio in real-time, giving you the ability to adapt and shape your music as you create.

Built-in Effects

Every channel in DWP-16 comes equipped with a suite of built-in effects that can be applied independently. Choose from delay, filter, reverb, flanger, chorus, phaser, and distortion to add depth, space, and dynamics to your sound. The effects are fully customizable, allowing you to tweak every parameter to match your creative vision. Whether you want subtle enhancements or dramatic transformations, the effects in DWP-16 give you the power to shape your audio precisely.

Advanced Modulation

DWP-16 features a comprehensive modulation system, including LFOs, curve sequencers, and random note generators. These tools allow you to add dynamic movement and variation to your sounds, making your music feel more alive and engaging. Drag-and-drop modulation routing makes it easy to connect modulation sources to any parameter, providing intuitive and creative control over your sound design.
