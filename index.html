Welcome to DWP-16

Professional 16-channel sampler with advanced sequencing and effects.

WIN: VST3 | Standalone MAC: VST3 | Standalone | AAX | AU

Advanced Multi-Channel Sampler

DWP-16 offers 16 independent channels, each equipped with comprehensive sample manipulation tools. Shape your sound with precision using FM synthesis, pitch control, panning, and gain adjustments. Process samples on-the-fly with instant reload capability, and utilize features like loop points and reverse playback for creative sound design.

Deep Sample Editing & Manipulation

Take control of your samples at the waveform level with precise editing tools. Fine-tune your sound using sample-accurate cutting and trimming, while the dynamic LFO line editor lets you draw custom modulation curves directly on the waveform. Create unique pitch variations and gain envelopes by drawing automation curves, allowing for complex sound design from a single sample. All changes can be instantly processed and reloaded, making sound design both intuitive and efficient.

Comprehensive Modulation and Effects

Each channel comes equipped with 3 LFOs plus a combined LFO mode, alongside a professional effects suite including Robotizer, Distortion, Chorus, Phaser, Flanger, Filter, Delay, and Reverb. All effects can be modulated via sequencer, LFOs, and four XY control pads, providing ultimate control over your sound.

Powerful Per-Channel Sequencer

Each channel features a sophisticated 32-pattern sequencer with deep modulation capabilities. Control velocity, pitch, and length parameters, plus two custom modulation curves per pattern. The integrated glitch engine allows for buffer manipulation with adjustable pitch, panning, portamento, and reverse options, opening up endless creative possibilities.
