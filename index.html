About DWP-16

The DWP-16 is an advanced multi-channel sampler designed for music producers, DJs, and audio enthusiasts who want to elevate their creative abilities. With 16 independent channels, each packed with powerful features and tools, DWP-16 offers complete control over every sample, effect, and parameter. Whether you are crafting beats, creating complex soundscapes, or designing custom instruments, DWP-16 provides the flexibility and functionality you need to make your ideas a reality.

Powerful Sample Editing

Each of the 16 channels in the DWP-16 can hold a unique sample, allowing for complex and layered compositions. The advanced sample editing features include pitch shifting, reverse playback, looping, and even FM synthesis on the sample itself. These tools allow you to transform any sound into something entirely new and unexpected.

Built-in Effects

Every channel in DWP-16 comes equipped with a suite of built-in effects that can be applied independently. Choose from delay, filter, reverb, flanger, chorus, phaser, and distortion to add depth, space, and dynamics to your sound. The effects are fully customizable, allowing you to tweak every parameter to match your creative vision.
