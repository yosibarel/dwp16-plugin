About DWP-16

The DWP-16 is an advanced multi-channel sampler designed for music producers, DJs, and audio enthusiasts. With 16 independent channels, each offering unique effects and tools, you have complete control over your samples. Use pitch shifting, reverse playback, looping, and FM synthesis to manipulate your sound to perfection.

Powerful Sample Editing

Each channel supports advanced sample editing. Use the built-in tools to edit your samples, apply effects like delay, reverb, flanger, and distortion, and create unique compositions that stand out in your music.

Built-in Effects

DWP-16 features a comprehensive suite of effects, including delay, reverb, flanger, chorus, phaser, and distortion. Customize each effect to match your creative vision and bring your samples to life.
