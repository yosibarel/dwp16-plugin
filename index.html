About DWP-16

The DWP-16 is an advanced multi-channel sampler designed for music producers, DJs, and audio enthusiasts who want to elevate their creative abilities. With 16 independent channels, each packed with powerful features and tools, DWP-16 offers complete control over every sample, effect, and parameter.

Powerful Sample Editing

Each of the 16 channels in the DWP-16 can hold a unique sample, allowing for complex and layered compositions. Advanced features include pitch shifting, reverse playback, looping, and FM synthesis.

Built-in Effects

DWP-16 comes with built-in effects like delay, reverb, flanger, chorus, phaser, and distortion. Customize each effect to bring out the best in your sound and create unique audio experiences.
